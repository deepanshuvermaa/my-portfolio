A full-stack MERN-based SaaS platform for launching and managing Facebook advertising campaigns. Features comprehensive role-based access control (RBAC) and integrates directly with Facebook API for seamless campaign management. This platform reduced daily monotonous campaign setup tasks from 1.5 hours to just 6 minutes, resulting in a 60% increase in operational efficiency and profits. Built with scalability in mind to handle multiple users and concurrent campaign operations.
