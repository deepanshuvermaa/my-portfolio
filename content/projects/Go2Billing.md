A production-grade SaaS platform for billing and parking management, currently serving 100+ active users and generating recurring revenue. Built with React Native/React for the billing management system and Flutter for parking management. Features secure user authentication, RBAC, real-time billing workflows, business analytics dashboards, and cloud-based data synchronization. Designed as a lightweight alternative to traditional POS systems, enabling businesses to operate directly from existing devices without expensive hardware dependencies.
